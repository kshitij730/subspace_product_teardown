## Key Recommendations

### 1. Remove the Login Wall (P0)

**Problem**
Users must sign up before understanding the product's value.

**Recommendation**
Introduce an Explore Mode that allows visitors to browse subscription groups, savings opportunities, and marketplace listings before authentication.

**Expected Outcome**
Higher activation and conversion rates.

---

### 2. Build a Trust Layer (P0)

**Problem**
The platform relies heavily on group admins, creating trust and dispute issues.

**Recommendation**
Launch a platform-managed tier where payments, credentials, and dispute resolution are handled by Subspace.

**Expected Outcome**
Lower churn and improved user trust.

---

### 3. Launch a Student Growth Engine (P1)

**Problem**
Students are the ideal customer segment but are not actively targeted.

**Recommendation**
Create a dedicated campus program with ambassadors, referral loops, and college-specific communities.

**Expected Outcome**
Scalable and cost-efficient user acquisition.

---

### 4. Make the Negotiate API Visible (P1)

**Problem**
Subspace's strongest competitive advantage is hidden from users.

**Recommendation**
Add negotiation actions, savings dashboards, and visible proof of money saved.

**Expected Outcome**
Stronger differentiation and retention.

---

### 5. Expand Through ONDC (P2)

**Problem**
The marketplace is dominated by national subscription brands.

**Recommendation**
Partner with ONDC and onboard local recurring-service businesses.

**Expected Outcome**
Long-term marketplace defensibility and supply-side growth.
